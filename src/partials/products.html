    History of milkshake

    The term "milkshake" was first used in 1885 in England to refer to a cocktail of whiskey and eggnog (a sweet drink based on raw chicken eggs and milk). The milkshake we all know and love was only invented in 1922 by Walgreens, a small pharmacy chain in the United States.
